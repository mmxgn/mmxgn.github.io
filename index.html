Title: Hello, World!

Hi,

My name is Emmanouil Theofanis Chourdakis (Εμμανουήλ-Θεοφάνης Χουρδάκης) and I am an Electronic and Computer Engineer from Greece currently living in Trondheim, Norway with my better half Beici❤.

I work as a Research Engineer at Nomono where I design and evaluate algorithms for speech enhancement. Nowadays I focus mostly on Next Generation Audio (Dolby Atmos, MPEG-H).

I received my Dipl.-Eng from the Technical University of Crete, and both my MSc and PhD from the Queen Mary University of London. I have also briefly worked for BBC Audio R&D doing some cool stuff with Object-based audio and Machine Learning for Speech Intelligibility.

On my spare time I am trying my luck at music production and game design, to various degrees of success. I also love everything Sci-Fi and I am a gamer.
