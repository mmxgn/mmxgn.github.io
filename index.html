My name is Emmanouil Theofanis Chourdakis (Εμμανουήλ-Θεοφάνης Χουρδάκης) and I am an Electronic and Computer Engineer from Greece currently living in Stockholm, Sweden with my better half Beici❤.

I work as a Machine Learning Engineer at Epidemic Sound. I currently work as an individual contributor for TBA products.

Previously, I worked as an Audio Research Engineer at Nomono where I contributed in their next generation audio speech enhancement pipeline.

I received my Dipl.-Eng from the Technical University of Crete, and both my MSc and PhD from the Queen Mary University of London. I have also briefly worked for BBC Audio R&D doing some cool stuff with Object-based audio and Machine Learning for Speech Intelligibility.

On my spare time I am trying my luck at music production and game design, to various degrees of success. I also love everything Sci-Fi and I am a gamer.
